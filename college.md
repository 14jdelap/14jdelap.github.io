# Accidents of life

There are 4 accidents that have noticeably influenced who I am today:
1. Being born in a well-off and highly educated family
2. Studying 3rd grade in the US
2. Losing my best school friend in 6th grade
3. Going to college to London

They each affected me in very tangible ways:
1. There's no chance I would be anywhere near where I am today otherwise
2. It took me until 10th grade —and a summer of non-stop learning— to recover my competence in mathematics
3. My middle school years were difficult
4. I discovered my confidence and ambition

They were all accidents of life:
1. I could've been born in any other house in my neighborhood in Lima, or worse
2. My mother could have not studied her masters, and I could've been more proactive improving the skills I needed to work on
3. The family quarrels could've been avoided
4. My IB grades were much higher than the school had expected
